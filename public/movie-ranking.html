The best books according to LUKE

My list of most important books fro businessmen 2025

Blue Ocean Strategy

Blue Ocean Strategy emphasizes creating uncontested market space, shifting from competition to innovation, and unlocking new demand through differentiation.

Rich dad Poor dad

Rich Dad Poor Dad contrasts wealthy and poor mindsets, emphasizing financial education, investing, and asset-building to achieve financial independence.

Extreme Ownership

Extreme Ownership by Jocko Willink and Leif Babin teaches leadership principles from Navy SEALs, emphasizing accountability, discipline, and leading by example.
